I am a human-centered researcher, specializing in artificial intelligence, agile enterprise and design strategy. An advocate of evidence-based design, I focus on transforming the user experience through a holistic systems approach to research, testing, and prototyping, to deliver measurable business results.

With experience ranging from non-profits to start-ups to enterprise, my work is connected by a commitment to using a research-driven approach to solve problems for people. In my current role as a UX Researcher with ServiceNow, I work alongside designers, machine learning scientists and product managers, to identify the right problems to solve with AI and ultimately deliver AI solutions that augment human decisions while delivering positive systemic impact.

I also combine my love of design and interiors through my vintage store Wildfang Home. One of the things I am most proud of is raising over $25,000 USD for various BLM organizations, as well as a feature in Architecture Digest.
